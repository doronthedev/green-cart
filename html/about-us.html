About Us We are Doron and Raz — two business partners and friends who believe that everyone deserves access to good food, no matter their situation. Our journey began when we saw how many people around us were struggling to afford daily meals. Families, students, and elderly neighbors had to choose between paying bills and putting food on the table. That broke our hearts — and motivated us to take action. So we created a website with one clear mission: to make food affordable for everyone. We work hard to find and offer quality food at cheap prices. But this is about more than just saving money — it's about dignity, care, and community. We believe that food should bring people together, not push them apart. Every product we list, every price we lower, and every customer we serve reminds us why we started: because everyone deserves to eat well — without worry, without shame. This isn't just a business. It's a movement. And it's for you. With care, Doron & Raz
